Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: April 28, 2025

Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data from our service, please follow these steps:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to Account Settings
  3. Select "Delete My Account"
  4. Confirm deletion by entering your password

Alternatively, you can email us at carzone1806@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" including your username and email address.

What We Delete

Upon receiving your request, we will delete all your personal information including:

  • Account profile information
  • Vehicle listings
  • Messages and communications
  • Browsing history and preferences

Timeframe

Data deletion will be completed within 30 days of your request.

Contact

For any questions regarding data deletion, please contact us at carzone1806@gmail.com.
